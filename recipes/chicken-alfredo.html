Chicken Alfredo

Description

This Chicken Alfredo will is the most delicious pasta your taste buds will ever encounter!

If you love pasta, then don't skip out on this one! Follow the steps below and share your thoughts with us!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 1 to 1.5 pounds)
  • 8 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 to 1.5 cups of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: a pinch of garlic powder or 2 minced garlic cloves for added flavor
  • Chopped parsley (optional)
  • Additional grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Cooking oil or butter for cooking the chicken
