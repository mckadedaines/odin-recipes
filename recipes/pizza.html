Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish known for its versatility and delicious taste. It typically consists of a round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients.

Ingredients

Dough Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour or bread flour
  • Warm water
  • Yeast (active dry or instant)
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil

Tomato Sauce:

  • Canned tomatoes or tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Salt and pepper

Cheese:

  • Mozzarella (fresh or shredded)
  • Optional: Parmesan, cheddar, or other cheeses

Toppings:

  • Meats: Pepperoni, sausage, ham, etc.
  • vegetables: Mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.
  • Others: Pineapple, anchovies, artichokes, etc.
