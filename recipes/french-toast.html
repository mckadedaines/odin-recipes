French Toast

Description

French toast is a classic and popular breakfast dish made from sliced bread soaked in a mixture of eggs and milk, then fried until golden brown. It's known for its rich, custardy interior and slightly crisp exterior.

Ingredients

Bread:

  • Thick-sliced white bread, brioche, or challah

Egg Mixture:

  • Eggs
  • Milk or cream
  • Sugar or honey (optional)
  • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ground cinnamon (optional)

Cooking:

  • Butter or oil for frying

Toppings and Accompaniments:

  • Maple syrup, honey, or powdered sugar
  • Fresh fruits like berries or sliced bananas
  • Whipped cream or yogurt
  • Nuts or nut butter for extra crunch and flavor
